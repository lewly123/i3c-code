About Us

True healing considers the whole person and not just their parts.

SoulSupport empowers teenagers facing mental health challenges through a 24/7 AI-powered chatbot. We offer a safe, compassionate space for teens to express themselves, providing support, guidance, and access to helpful resources. Our mission is to ensure no teen feels alone, fostering resilience and well-being with innovation and care.

Why Choose Us

Struggling? We’re here for you 24/7. Our AI chatbot is here to help anytime—whether you’re dealing with midnight anxiety or just need someone to listen. It’s private, judgment-free, and easy to use, with no login required and instant responses to your questions. Get tips on managing stress, resources like relaxation exercises, and strategies to improve your mental well-being that fit your needs. Thinking about professional help? We’ll guide you there too. Simple, affordable, and designed for everyone—we’re here to make mental health support easy and accessible.
